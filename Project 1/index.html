About Me

I am currently working towards a degree in computer science at Oregon State University, alongside my existing degree in kinesiology. This dual educational background allows me to offer a unique blend of biomechanics and technology expertise. In addition to my professional experiences, I have displayed leadership skills as the Director of Administrative Affairs for OSU's student government. While I graduated Summa Cum Laude with a B.Sc. in Kinesiology in 2022, my primary focus now is on pursuing my computer science degree at OSU. Proficient in various software, including Microsoft Office, Canva, and specialized biomechanics programs, I am excited to leverage my diverse background in a role that intersects sports, technology, and research.
